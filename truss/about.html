About Us
A Truss is an important structure type in structural engineering. Our project aims to solve such structures, and to assess their stability. We've used equilibrium of forces in x-direction(horizontal) and y-direction(vertical) for every joint in the truss system and have solved this system of linear equations by transforming the equation matrix into upper triangular matrix and performing back-substitution to get the force on each member. This project was completed under the guidance of Shubhamoy Sen.
